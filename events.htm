Events

  • Midnight Madness Adoption October 3, 2016
    • Volunteers and staff will wear their flannel pajamas, fuzzy slippers and comfy robes or the attire of their favorite sports team. The shelter will remain open until midnight for adoptions, with a special adopathon price of $25.00 to help homeless animals find loving furever homes.
  • Top Dog October 10, 2016
    • Entries will be accepted starting on September 10th with voting to open on October 1st. Submit a picture to wths@gmail.com or at the shelter with a $5 entry fee for the dog you think has what it takes to lead the "pack" at our 10th annual Puppy Palooza on October 24, 2016.
    • Come celebrate at 6 p.m. at the shelter to see who the winner is! There will be food, drinks, and door prizes!
  • Cool Kat October 17, 2016
    • The event will be held at the shelter at 6 p.m. and will feature more than 25 homeless cats and kittens up for adoption.
    • There will be food, drinks, and door prizes to celebrate finding our wonderful and loving cats furever homes!
  • Puppy Palooza October 24, 2016
    • The event will be held at the shelter at 6 p.m. and will feature more than 50 homeless dogs and puppies up for adoption.
    • There will be food, drinks, and door prizes to celebrate finding these sweet pups furever homes!
  • CatKnits November 1, 2016
    • Come and Sit & Knit with our cats and kittens in the cat room while spending time and loving on these sweet kitties!
    • Event will start at 3 p.m. and last until 6 p.m.
  • Paws and Relax November 8, 2016
    • Come and enjoy some relaxation time with our specially trained therapy dogs!
    • The event will take place at the shelter in our Meet 'n Greet room and will begin at 3 p.m. and last until 6 p.m.
Make sure to stay updated on what's going on around the shelter by visiting the site regularly as times and dates may change. You may subscribe to our newsletter to get regular emails on events and new information being posted on the site.
