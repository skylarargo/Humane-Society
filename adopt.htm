Adopt a Pet

How to Adopt:

If you are interested in adopting a pet, please call 863-647-2237, come by the shelter, or email wths@gmail.com.

There are few guidelines that we have before we allow you to adopt a pet:

  • No chains or runlines are acceptable.
  • Dogs must be kept inside or in a fenced yard.
  • Cats are almost always placed as indoor pets.
  • WTHS has the right to deny any adoptions.
  • You must be 18+ years of age to adopt a pet.
  • We can email or fax an application
